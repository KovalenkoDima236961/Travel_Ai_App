# Paris budget notes

- Many churches, gardens, riverside walks, and neighborhood viewpoints are free.
- Picnic lunches from bakeries or markets can keep costs down without losing local flavor.
- Museum passes only help when travelers plan enough paid museum visits in a short window.
- Staying slightly outside the most central arrondissements can reduce hotel costs if transit access is good.
- Book timed tickets for major attractions to avoid wasting paid itinerary time in lines.
