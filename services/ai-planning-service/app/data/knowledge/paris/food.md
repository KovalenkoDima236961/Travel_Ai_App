# Paris food tips

- Reserve popular bistros in advance, especially for dinner and weekends.
- Neighborhood bakeries are useful for breakfast; try croissants, baguette sandwiches, and seasonal pastries.
- Avoid restaurants with large multilingual menus directly facing major monuments.
- Rue Montorgueil, Marché des Enfants Rouges, and neighborhood markets can work well for food-focused walks.
- Leave time for a relaxed cafe stop rather than scheduling every meal as a formal restaurant.
