# Rome hidden gems

- The Orange Garden on the Aventine Hill has a calm city view and pairs well with the nearby keyhole viewpoint.
- Quartiere Coppedè is useful for unusual architecture when travelers want something beyond ancient Rome.
- Centrale Montemartini mixes classical sculpture with an old power station setting.
- Garbatella works well for a slower neighborhood walk with local residential character.
- Early mornings around smaller churches and side streets are better than midday near headline landmarks.
