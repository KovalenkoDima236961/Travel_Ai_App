# Rome food tips

- Avoid restaurants directly beside the Colosseum, Trevi Fountain, and Spanish Steps because prices are usually higher.
- Try carbonara, cacio e pepe, amatriciana, supplì, maritozzo, and Roman-style pizza al taglio.
- Trastevere is useful for evening food, but the most central streets can still be touristy.
- Testaccio is a strong area for traditional Roman food and market-based stops.
- Look for small trattorias a few streets away from major landmarks.
